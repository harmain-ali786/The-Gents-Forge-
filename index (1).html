Forging Excellence Since 1985

The Gents Forge was born from a passion for craftsmanship and timeless style. Our founder, James Harrington, set out to create clothing that would stand the test of time - both in quality and design.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously crafted using the finest materials and traditional techniques, updated for the modern gentleman. We believe in slow fashion - creating fewer, better pieces that become wardrobe staples.
